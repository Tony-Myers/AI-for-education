Featured Tool: NotebookLM

NotebookLM (Google)

Essential for Staff & Students
Launch Tool →

A "grounded" AI assistant that processes text, audio, and video sources. Unlike standard chatbots, it cites its sources, making it safer for academic environments. It has recently evolved into a multimodal powerhouse capable of generating podcasts, visual aids, and lesson resources.

Verification Required

While using uploaded sources lowers the risk of hallucination compared to the open web, it does not eliminate it. You must always check the citation links provided next to every claim to ensure the AI has interpreted your source text accurately.

For Teaching & Support

  • 🎙️ Generate "Audio Overviews" (Podcasts): Convert your text-heavy module handbooks or lecture notes into engaging audio discussions for accessibility.
  • 📹 Video Processing: Upload YouTube lecture links. Ask NotebookLM to summarise them or extract key quotes for your slides.
  • 🧩 Instant Quiz & Flashcards: Generate 20-question quizzes or flashcard tables from your readings to upload to the VLE.
  • 🎞️ Slide Deck & Infographic Outlines: Ask the AI to "Outline a 5-slide deck" or "Plan an infographic timeline" based on your sources.

For Research & Admin

  • 🔍 Grounded Analysis: Upload 20 PDFs and ask: "Synthesise the arguments on X across all sources with citations."
  • 🧠 Mind Mapping: Use it to identify connections between varied sources to structure your literature review.
  • 📝 Grant & Briefing Prep: Quickly generate briefing documents, FAQs, or timelines from rough notes.
